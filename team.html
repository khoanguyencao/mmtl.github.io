Our Team

The Monash Medical Technology Lab (MMTL) was founded in 2024 and is directed by Associate Professor Khoa N. Cao. Khoa trained in medicine at Monash University and Austin Health, public health at the University of London (LSHTM) and engineering at Stanford University as an Fulbright Scholar, where he graduated from the world-renowned Stanford Biodesign program.

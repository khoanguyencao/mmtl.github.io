Medical Technology
Strategy Masterclasses

As a natural follow-on to our Foundations of Medical Technology Innovation course, we have launched a series of advanced masterclasses to introduce Australian innovators to the third and final stage of Biodesign - implementation.

The implementation stage requires innovators to assess if the "stars align" on their project, namely that their clinical, technical and operational strategy will provide them sufficient opportunity to succeed. Note that these considerations should be completed before significant time, money and effort are expended as the problem and solution usually need to evolve as the strategy develops (i.e. the first problem/solution combination is rarely a home run on every front).

What will the masterclasses cover?

  • Regulatory Strategy: What are the main regulatory bodies globally? How do they operate? Does my product require regulatory approval? If so, how do I determine my product classification? How do I craft my regulatory strategy?
  • Intellectual Property Strategy: What are the main concepts I need to know about IP and patents? How do I assess freedom-to-operate and patentability? How do I capture my invention? How do I deal with patent attorneys?
  • Commercialisation Strategy: How do I think about commercialisation? What business models should I consider?
  • Reimbursement Strategy: How does reimbursement work? What is coding, coverage and payment? How do I craft my reimbursement strategy?
  • Operating Strategy: How does venture capital work? How do I plan my exit strategy? How should I deal with equity and investors? How do I bring everything together into a coherent roadmap?

    • We keep masterclasses small to ensure quality of instruction.

    Are there any pre-requisites?
    We assume that you have completed the Foundations of Medical Technology Innovation course or otherwise hold equivalent knowledge. You do not need to have a project to attend the masterclass.

    How do I sign-up?
    We are currently piloting our masterclasses on an invite-only basis at Monash. We expect to launch a paid version of the masterclasses to our clinical/industry partners mid-late 2024 and to the broader public in 2025. We do have some exceptions to this rule. If you are interested in attending these masterclasses, please contact us.

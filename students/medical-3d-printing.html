Medical 3D Printing
Certificate

The Monash Medical Technology Lab will hold a medical 3D printing certificate for pre-clinical medical students at Monash from Monday July 8th to Friday July 19th during the winter break.

The course will cover the foundations of computer-aided design, designing for 3D printing, FDM/SLS manufacturing, materials, connections and post-processing. The certificate will culminate in a capstone where students will design and print their own product.

The course will be capped at 15 students. Applications will open in April. Auditors welcome.
